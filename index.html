Who am I?

Hi! I'm Dani, a software engineer with over five years of professional experience in the industry. I'm an avid Rustacean with interests including cryptography, cybersecurity, distributed systems, database internals, network programming, and more. I've been programming since I was a young child and am interested in every niche technology I encounter.

While my professional experience is mostly with Rust, I'm a huge nerd for programming of all sorts, having also studied languages including but not limited to:

  • C
  • C++
  • C#
  • Gleam
  • Go
  • Haskell
  • Java
  • Kotlin
  • Scala
  • Standard ML
  • Python

Beyond the Turing complete, I am also experienced with DevOps-related technologies like K8s, GitHub Actions, Terraform, etc. I also have fun doing capture the flag and am currently studying to take some certifications for pentesting and red teaming.

Outside of technology, my interests are as varied as 3D printing, collecting fountain pens, locksport, juggling, and learning languages.

I'm always open to hearing about new opportunities, so feel free to contact me if you think I might be a good fit for something.
